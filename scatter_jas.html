Scatter Plot: Renewable Energy vs CO₂ Emissions

This scatter plot shows the relationship between renewable energy share and CO₂ emissions per person. Colors represent continents, and point size represents electricity consumption. It helps identify which countries have higher emissions and how renewable usage is related.

Optimized Scatter Plot (2024 Only, Log CO₂ Scale)

This optimized version filters the data to 2024 and applies a log scale on CO₂ emissions. This reduces clutter and makes patterns clearer, highlighting the most recent renewable energy trends.
